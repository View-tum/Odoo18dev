Few Words Salesperson Signature & Stamp

This module gives you option to show salesperson signature and company stamp on the sale order, invoice and delivery orders. By using the signature widget users and add their signature.

Main features:

  • Add company stamp.
  • Set the position of company stamp.
  • Add or draw salesperson signature.
  • Signature and stamp used in sale reports.
  • Signature and stamp used in inventory/stock reports.
  • Signature and stamp used in account/invoicing reports.
  • Signature and stamp used in Purchase reports.
