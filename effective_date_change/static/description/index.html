Overview

This module will allows you to change the effective date upon receiving or sending (or also called picking process).
You can also change the effective date of picking process that is alread in "Done" state.

Update Sales Order & Purchase Order

Change effective date on sales order & purchase order.

Update Stock Moves

The stock moves will automatically adjust their date to match the effective date you select.

Support Foreign Currency

It will dynamically adjust the currency rate according to the exchange rate of the selected historical date.

The Stock Valuation Will Also Follow

If you set automated stock valuation, it will also follow the new date you choose.

Best Suits For Your Late Document Input

If you need to work on a past document or record today, this module will seamlessly adjust the picking-related data to reflect the past date.
